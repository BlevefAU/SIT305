App title:
Knight Principle



App Overview:
This application is a RPG game develop for android using android studio. In this game, you are request to play as a man who just wake up from unconscious and start looking for your memorise.


Major features:
1. The game have three different ending, so player should try to seek out all the requestment to get all the endings
2. There are three small game in this game, dice, hunting and finishing.
3. If player doing some right choice in the game, they will increase love level with another NPC so that will make the content become a bit different.
